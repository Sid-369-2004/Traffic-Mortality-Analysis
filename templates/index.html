TrafficMortality Analysis

An advanced AI-based traffic severity prediction system analyzing weather factors, road infrastructure, and spatial intelligence.

System Features

The machine learning model analyzes 11 critical environmental and road factors to predict accidents.

  • Weather Factors: Temperature, Humidity, Pressure, Visibility.
  • Dynamic Conditions: Wind Speed, Time of Day (Daylight / Nighttime).
  • Location & Infrastructure: Latitude, Longitude, Crossings, Junctions, Traffic Signals.

Critical Severity Thresholds Guide

The AI actively flags parameters exceeding the following historic baselines as high-risk triggers for severe fatal accidents:

Visibility Range < 3.0 miles
Wind Velocity > 15.0 mph
Temperature < 32.0°F (Freeze)
Infrastructure Junctions / Crossings

Severity Scale

How the model categorizes risk into a simple binary outcome:


Non-Severe

Routine incidents, minor property damage, overall minimal traffic delay.

Severe

Major accidents involving injuries, fatalities, or complete road closures.
